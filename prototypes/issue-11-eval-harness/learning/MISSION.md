# Mission: Read and design OpenMagic evaluations

## Why

Learn to explain and defend how OpenMagic distinguishes product correctness,
agent quality, recovery safety, and live-provider evidence during a senior AI
engineering interview.

## Success looks like

- Classify an evaluation observation as a gate, diagnostic, or smoke proof.
- Explain why the inherited baseline is measured but does not control V0 safety.
- Read an evaluation scenario and identify its perturbation and required evidence.

## Constraints

- Learn through the concrete OpenMagic renewal-email tracer.
- Keep the first lesson short and interactive.

## Out of scope

- Statistical model-release thresholds and generalized evaluation platforms.
